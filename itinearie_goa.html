Recommended Goa Itineraries

2-Day Goa Getaway

Day 1

  • Morning: Arrive and relax at Calangute Beach
  • Afternoon: Visit Fort Aguada
  • Evening: Enjoy beachside dinner at Baga

Day 2

  • Morning: Explore Old Goa Churches
  • Afternoon: Spice plantation tour
  • Evening: Sunset cruise on Mandovi River

4-Day Goa Adventure

Day 1

  • Morning: Arrive and relax at your resort
  • Afternoon: North Goa beach hopping (Calangute, Baga, Anjuna)
  • Evening: Seafood dinner at beach shack

Day 2

  • Morning: Visit Aguada Fort and Chapora Fort
  • Afternoon: Vagator Beach
  • Evening: Party at a beach club

Day 3

  • Morning: Explore Old Goa (Se Cathedral, Basilica of Bom Jesus)
  • Afternoon: Visit a spice plantation
  • Evening: Mandovi River cruise

Day 4

  • Morning: Shopping at Panaji
  • Afternoon: Visit Dona Paula viewpoint
  • Evening: Depart from Goa
