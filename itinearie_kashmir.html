Recommended Kashmir Itineraries

3-Day Kashmir Escape

Day 1: Srinagar Serenity

  • Morning: Arrival & check-in at a houseboat
  • Afternoon: Shikara ride on Dal Lake
  • Evening: Visit Mughal Gardens (Nishat & Shalimar)

Day 2: Gulmarg Adventure

  • Morning: Drive to Gulmarg
  • Afternoon: Gondola ride & snow activities
  • Evening: Return to Srinagar

Day 3: Local Delights

  • Morning: Visit Hazratbal Shrine & Jamia Masjid
  • Afternoon: Shop for handicrafts and dry fruits
  • Evening: Departure

5-Day Kashmir Retreat

Day 1: Arrival in Srinagar

  • Morning: Arrive and relax at a houseboat
  • Afternoon: Shikara ride on Dal Lake
  • Evening: Explore Nishat Bagh and Shalimar Bagh

Day 2: Gulmarg Getaway

  • Morning: Scenic drive to Gulmarg
  • Afternoon: Gondola ride and skiing
  • Evening: Return to Srinagar

Day 3: Pahalgam Paradise

  • Morning: Drive to Pahalgam
  • Afternoon: Visit Betaab Valley & Aru Valley
  • Evening: Overnight in Pahalgam

Day 4: Scenic Drive Back

  • Morning: Enjoy Lidder River activities
  • Afternoon: Return to Srinagar
  • Evening: Shop at Lal Chowk

Day 5: Culture & Departure

  • Morning: Visit Hazratbal Shrine
  • Afternoon: Local craft museum or floating market
  • Evening: Departure
