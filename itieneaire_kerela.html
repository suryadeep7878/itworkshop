Recommended Kerala Itineraries

2-Day Kerala Glimpse

Day 1: Cochin City & Culture

  • Morning: Visit Fort Kochi & Chinese Fishing Nets
  • Afternoon: Explore Mattancherry Palace & Jewish Synagogue
  • Evening: Kathakali performance and local dinner

Day 2: Backwater Bliss

  • Morning: Drive to Alleppey
  • Afternoon: Houseboat cruise with lunch
  • Evening: Sunset over the backwaters & departure

5-Day Kerala Discovery

Day 1: Cochin Arrival & Heritage

  • Morning: Arrive & explore Fort Kochi
  • Afternoon: Visit Dutch Palace & Jewish Town
  • Evening: Watch Kathakali at a cultural center

Day 2: Munnar Hills

  • Morning: Drive to Munnar
  • Afternoon: Visit Tea Museum & spice plantations
  • Evening: Relax at scenic viewpoint

Day 3: Munnar Exploration

  • Morning: Eravikulam National Park
  • Afternoon: Mattupetty Dam & Echo Point
  • Evening: Local food experience

Day 4: Alleppey Houseboat

  • Morning: Drive to Alleppey
  • Afternoon: Check-in to houseboat, cruise through backwaters
  • Evening: Overnight on houseboat

Day 5: Departure

  • Morning: Houseboat checkout & travel back to Cochin
  • Afternoon: Shopping at Lulu Mall or local markets
  • Evening: Departure

8-Day Kerala Grand Tour

Day 1: Cochin Welcome

  • Morning: Arrive in Cochin, check-in
  • Afternoon: Fort Kochi sightseeing
  • Evening: Kathakali & seafood dinner

Day 2: Athirappilly Falls

  • Morning: Day trip to Athirappilly Waterfalls
  • Afternoon: Picnic by the falls
  • Evening: Return to Cochin

Day 3: Drive to Munnar

  • Morning: Scenic drive through hills
  • Afternoon: Tea museum & spice plantations
  • Evening: Sunset at Pothamedu Viewpoint

Day 4: Munnar Full Day

  • Morning: Eravikulam National Park
  • Afternoon: Mattupetty Dam & boating
  • Evening: Local cuisine experience

Day 5: Thekkady Jungle

  • Morning: Travel to Thekkady
  • Afternoon: Periyar Wildlife Sanctuary boat safari
  • Evening: Martial arts or tribal dance show

Day 6: Alleppey Houseboat

  • Morning: Drive to Alleppey
  • Afternoon: Board houseboat & cruise
  • Evening: Overnight stay on the backwaters

Day 7: Kovalam Beach

  • Morning: Travel to Kovalam
  • Afternoon: Relax at Lighthouse Beach
  • Evening: Sunset by the Arabian Sea

Day 8: Trivandrum & Departure

  • Morning: Visit Padmanabhaswamy Temple & Napier Museum
  • Afternoon: Last-minute shopping
  • Evening: Departure from Trivandrum
